Are you a math teacher?

Need problem data for teaching?

SoulMath's Math Problem Collection is the solution.

We provide 738 math problems that can be random numbers, random images, and random answer choices, along with answer keys.

So, a single problem can have multiple variations of numbers, images, and answer choices each time you load the same problem. This prevents monotony and allows students to practice and monitor their mastery of a problem.

To create your own version of the problem, contact us by email.
